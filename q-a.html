How long does the curing process take for your ham ?

Our ham goes through a long curing process that can take up to three years to achieve the perfect flavor and texture. This slow curing process is a traditional method that has been passed down through generations, and it results in a product that is unparalleled in taste and quality.

What makes your ham so special ?

Our ham is made from Iberian pigs raised on our acorn-rich farm and processed in Jabugo, a region known for its expertise in curing ham. The unique microclimate and soil composition in Jabugo provides the perfect environment for producing top-quality ham.

What kind of pigs do you raise ?

At El Rincon, we raise Iberian pigs along with other crossbreeds such as 75% or 50% Iberian pigs in order the keep the diversity and strive to provide the best quality products. Of course some white pigs, Duroc, are the kinds we raise. Our pigs are raised on acorn-rich farms and are protected by the Spanish government.

What is the difference between Iberian ham and regular ham ?

Iberian ham is made from a specific breed of pig and raised on a diet of acorns, giving it a unique flavor and texture. The curing process also differs, resulting in a ham with a nutty, rich taste.

What types of iberian pork products does El Rincon offer ?

El Rincon offers a variety of iberian pork products, including Jamon Iberico de Bellota, Lomo Iberico, Chorizo Iberico, and more. Each product is made from high-quality iberian pork and crafted using traditional techniques to ensure the best possible flavor and texture.

What makes iberian pork special and different from other types of meat ?

Iberian pork is considered special due to its unique flavor, tender texture, and high quality. The pigs are raised on a diet of acorns and grass, which gives the meat a distinct nutty and sweet taste.

How should I store iberian pork products ?

Iberian pork products should be stored in a cool, dry place, away from light and moisture. They can be kept in the refrigerator, but should be removed from the refrigerator at least 30 minutes before eating to allow the meat to reach room temperature.

Can I store my ham for a long time ?

Ham from El Rincon is best consumed within 7-10 days of opening the packaging, and up to 2 weeks with proper storage. Storing ham for an extended period of time may affect its taste and quality.

What is the best way to wrap my Iberian ham from El Rincon for storage ?

The best way to wrap your ham is to use a clean, dry cloth, or alternatively, you can use a vacuum-sealed bag to ensure the ham stays fresh and protected from air exposure.

What is the best way to store fresh pork from El Rincon ?

Fresh pork should be stored in the refrigerator at a temperature between 0-4°C (32-40°F) and consumed within 3-5 days. If you plan on keeping it for a longer period, it can be stored in the freezer at a temperature below -18°C (0°F) for up to 6 months.

How should I thaw frozen fresh pork from El Rincon ?

The best way to thaw frozen fresh pork is to place it in the refrigerator overnight, allowing it to thaw slowly. You can also thaw it in cold water, but make sure to change the water every 30 minutes to prevent the growth of bacteria.

Can I store my ham in the refrigerator ?

Yes, you can store your ham in the refrigerator, but make sure to wrap it properly to prevent it from drying out. To maintain the optimal temperature and moisture levels, it is best to place the ham in a specially designed ham holder or in a well-sealed container. But, freezing ham is not recommended as it can affect the texture and taste of the product. The change in temperature and moisture can cause the ham to lose its natural flavor and texture. It's best to store the ham in a cool, dry place and consume it within the recommended time frame.

What is the philosophy of El Rincon ?

At El Rincon, we believe in responsible and sustainable farming practices, as well as preserving the traditional methods of producing Iberian ham. Our philosophy is to provide our customers with the highest-quality products while also taking care of our pigs and the environment.

What are the qualifications of El Rincon's products ?

Our products are made from 100% Iberian pigs, raised in their natural habitat and fed with a diet rich in acorns. All of our products are certified by the Denomination of Origin, ensuring that they meet the strict standards set by the Spanish government.

What makes El Rincon's products unique compared to other brands ?

El Rincon's Iberian pork products are unique due to our commitment to tradition and authenticity. We use traditional methods and work closely with a factory in Jabugo, known for its expertise in ham production, to ensure that our products meet the highest standards. Additionally, our pigs are raised in the Dehesa region, where they are fed a diet rich in acorns, contributing to the unique flavor of our products.

What are the production processes of El Rincon ?

Our Iberian pigs are raised in their natural habitat in the Dehesa, where they feed on a diet rich in acorns. After slaughter, the hams are salted and then undergo a long curing process that can last up to 36 months. During this time, the hams are dried, matured, and rubbed with a special blend of oils and spices to enhance their flavor. Only after passing a series of rigorous quality control tests are they finally packaged and ready for sale.

What makes El Rincon's Iberian pork products special ?

El Rincon's Iberian pork products are made from acorn-fed Iberian pigs raised on our farm in Almadende la Plata, Spain. Our pigs are protected by the Spanish government, and we work with the prestigious ham factory in Jabugo to produce top-quality jamon. The microclimate and soil composition of the area, combined with the expertise of our producer, result in ham with a unique taste and texture.

What is El Rincon's approach to sustainability ?

At El Rincon, we understand the importance of preserving the environment for future generations. We take a holistic approach to sustainability, from the way we raise our pigs to the packaging we use for our products. We are committed to reducing our carbon footprint and minimizing waste, and we work closely with suppliers to ensure that our products are produced in an environmentally responsible manner.
