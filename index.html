04 / Care Our Environment

We promote the concept of sustainability through sustainable farming practices. Free-ranging method to improve animal welfare, enhance meat quality, and reduce environmental impact.
Pigs that are raised on a free-range diet may have better meat quality, as they are able to move around, exercise and have access to a varied diet, which can improve the marbling and flavor of the meat.
In order to prevent genetic diseases, crossbreeding is important, where different breeds of pigs are mated to create offspring with a combination of desired traits from both parents.
